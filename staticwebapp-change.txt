staticwebapp.config.json — change
==================================

Open your existing staticwebapp.config.json. In the "routes" array, add one
entry for /templates.html. It should sit alongside your existing page routes
(next to the /manager.html or /projects.html entry):

    {
      "route": "/templates.html",
      "allowedRoles": ["anonymous"]
    }

If your config uses a "navigationFallback" block that excludes specific paths
(so deep links don't fall through to index.html), add "/templates.html" to
that exclusion list too — same place as /manager.html.

No other changes to the config are needed. Microsoft login is triggered from
shared.js at runtime, not enforced at the route level, matching the other pages.
