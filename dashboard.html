📑 Quote Overview

Every quote & tender at a glance — edit live where a quote exists
ℹ️ How to read this

This is a live view of every quote and tender for the year — it updates itself from the system, so there's nothing to keep in sync by hand.

Quoting? — Yes means a quote already exists in the Quote Builder. No means it's still just a tender that hasn't been turned into a quote yet.

Row colours follow the key just below this panel — same idea as the old spreadsheet. The one to watch is red (overdue): due date passed and not yet sent, won or lost. Lost and closed-off jobs go grey with the quote number crossed out, deliberately kept separate from red so the two never get mixed up.

Editing. On rows where Quoting? = Yes, the Done by, Chasing, WIN and Comments cells can be changed directly — just click and type or pick. Changes save straight away and show up on the Quotes tab too. Tender-only rows (Quoting? = No) can't be edited here until they've been opened in the Quote Builder.

Use the search box and filters to narrow things down, click a column heading to sort, and ⬇ Export CSV to pull the current view out to a spreadsheet.
